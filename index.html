  • e-Scape 19

    Department of Electronics and Communication, College of Engineering Trivandrum presents e-Scape the first edition of its Tech Fest on 27th and 28th April 2019.

  • E-scape is meant to be a break, an “escape” from the normal routine of classroom learning

    By providing opportunities to make, break and explore through a wide variety of events, this melting pot of ideas will provide the means for students to interact with people outside their classrooms and meet people with contrasting views and vastly different definitions of creativity

  • Planned are plenty of talks that give students insights into the whats, hows and whys of the latest developments in technology

    Our workshops are also sure to leave students all the more geekier. Also in store for participants are contests and challenges to prove their mettle and win exciting prizes. Last, but definitely not the least, what better way to end the day than with the array of informals that we have planned out?

Mobile Site Making

handled by Entrench Electronics in association with IIT Bombay + Google Certification

All of us might have experienced a situation wherein we badly wanted to check out a site,but it kept displaying improperly.Like a half baked cake.

Tired with websites that open only on PC's and that are incompatible with mobile phones?

Maybe this time, rather than ranting about what we don't have, why can't we just create a solution?

Department of Electronics and Communication, CET as part of e-Scape , first edition of its tech fest,is here to help you with this dilemma. Get ready and prep yourself to attend our workshop this e-Scape.

Handled by Entrench Electronics in association with IIT Bombay, this workshop on Mobile Site Making with Google Certification will be conducted on April 27th and 28th.

Registration fee: ₹1200 per head (Food and refreshments included)

Date : April 27th and 28th 2019
