Workshop on open IOT using LoRa

Don't miss out on this opportunity to acquaint yourself with this game changing technology

e-Scape19, the technical fest of Department of Electronics and Communication, CET, presents to you a one - day workshop on emerging digital wireless data communication technology 'LoRa'

LoRa is one of the latest technologies in the communications industry. It is a groundbreaking tech and one that will surely usher in a new paradigm in industries like Internet of Things and connected smart homes.

In addition to the benefits of long range and low power consumption it is an open protocol and anyone can set up their own private infrastructure. This makes LoRa a game changer in our smart world connected by IOT.

Registration fee: ₹599 per head

Date : April 27th 2019
